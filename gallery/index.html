Dreeemy

and when they speak of the force of nature
that brought the world back
when she was at her brink
they will speak of love.
They will speak of art.
and they will speak of us —
of our people.
The ones who kept showing up.
They will tell their children's children:
When it feels like there is absolutely nothing ...

Love Is Everything.
